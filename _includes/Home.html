LatinX in AI (LXAI) Research Journal

This is a publication of peer-reviewed accepted research papers for official LatinX in AI (LXAI) workshops co-located with prestigious AI, ML, CV, NLP, Robotics, and Data Science conferences held globally and virtually.

Learn more about the LatinX in AI Org, our official research workshops, and other programs on our main website https://www.latinxinai.org/
